About Me

Hello! I’m an associate professor in the Department of Statistics, Faculty of Arts and Sciences, Beijing Normal University. My research interests focus on the mathematical foundations of machine learning and artificial intelligence. My previous work has concentrated on optimization theory, manifold learning, matrix factorization, dimensionality reduction, and lifelong learning.
