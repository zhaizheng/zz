About Me

Hello! I'm an Associate Professor in the Department of Statistics, Faculty of Arts and Sciences, Beijing Normal University. My research lies at the intersection of mathematics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on developing mathematically grounded algorithms supported by rigorous theoretical analysis. My previous work has focused on optimization on manifolds, matrix factorization, manifold learning, dimensionality reduction, and lifelong learning. More recently, my research has expanded to reinforcement learning and diffusion models, with an emphasis on efficient optimization, representation learning, and scalable generative modeling.
